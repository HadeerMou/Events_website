Soul Concert

Sakia Abdel Monem El Sawy Cultural Center will host a concert by the "Soul" band led by Dr. Samah Ismail on its stage at the Nile Hall at 7:00 PM today, Saturday. The ticket price is 100 Egyptian pounds. It is worth noting that the "Soul" band consists of a selected group of voices that have been trained by Dr. Samah Ismail, a professor in the Department of Arabic Music at the Faculty of Music Education. The band is expected to perform a diverse selection of beautiful songs during the concert, engaging the audience with their performance on stage.

Get ready for an unforgettable experience and join us in soul concert

Date : Nov 5, 2024

Time : 7:00 pm - 10:00 pm

Address : 3668+VJ6, Al Gabalayah, Zamalek, Cairo Governorate 4270110, Egypt
